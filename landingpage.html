Urban Bites is a revered azal-based diner from Dex Restaurant Concepts. Built on the idea of reinventing the place where continental meals are served, this neighborhood eatery satisfies diners with imaginative twists. Urban Bites has been serving the African community and more for nearly one hundred years.
